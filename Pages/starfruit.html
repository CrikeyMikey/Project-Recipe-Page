Icy Carambola

An ice beverage that is a blend of apple, pear, and citrues fruit flavors

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 4 carambolas (about 1 1/4 pounds) cut into 1/2 inch slices
  • 3 tablespoons ginger syrup
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 2 cups crushed ice
  • 1/2 cups lemon-lime flavored sparkling water
  • Carambola slices

How to make:

1. Place pineapple juice and carambola slices in a blender; process until smooth. Pour mixture through a sieve into a pitcher to measure 2 cups. Discard solids. Stir in ginger syrup and lemon juice. Cover and refrigerate 45 minutes or until thoroughly chilled.

2. Fill each of 2 glasses with 1 cup crushed ice. Pour star fruit mixture over ice. Add 1/4 cup sparkling water to each glass. Garnish with carambola slices, if desired.
