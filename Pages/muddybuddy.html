Muddy Buddies

Classic chocolate, peanut butter, and sugary Chex mix snack

Ingredients:

  • 9 cups of Chex cereal
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cups peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

How to make:

1. Into large bowl, measure cereal; set aside

2. In 1 quart microwaveable bowl, microwave the chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter on High for 1 min and stir. Microwave for roughly 30 seconds longer, stir in vanilla. Pour mix onto cereal, stirring until evenly coated. Pout into 2-gallon resealable bag.

3. Add powdered sugar. Seal bag and shake until well coated. Spread on waxed paper to cool. Store in refrigerator.
